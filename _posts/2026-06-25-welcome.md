This site was created to make information about the Parker-Damon school transition and developing PTO easier for parents and caregivers to find.

## What this site will provide

- Short dated updates
- A summary of what is known, proposed, or still unknown
- Publicly announced dates
- Links to official sources
- A way for parents to submit questions
- Publicly confirmed PTO board and organizing roles

## What this site is not

This is not an official communication channel of the PTO, Parker-Damon school administration, Acton-Boxborough Regional School District, or the School Committee.

Whenever possible, updates will distinguish among:

- **Confirmed:** supported by an official public source
- **Under discussion:** proposed but not finalized
- **Not yet decided:** no final decision has been publicly announced

Official communications and source documents take precedence over summaries presented here.
